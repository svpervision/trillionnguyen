NDV Performance

A bold, dark-palette website redesign for a performance training brand — dynamic, fast, and built to reflect their identity.

RoleProduct Designer
CompanyFreelance
Year2019
Scope of WorkBrand Design · UX Design · UI Design · Responsive Layout · Content Strategy
Problem 01

The Challenge

This project demanded a website redesign that pulsed with the high-impact, dynamic energy of the client's fast-paced product. My mission was to craft an engaging experience that reflects their unique brand identity.

Crafting a website that seamlessly adapts to diverse content volumes presented an exciting challenge. Without finalized copy and direction, I couldn't predict the exact content amount — so I designed a dynamic layout that could effortlessly scale up or down without compromising aesthetics or user experience.

Without finalized copy, I designed a dynamic layout that could scale up or down without compromising aesthetics or user experience.

Discovery 02

Understanding the Problem

Two core challenges defined the project. First: limited information during wireframing meant designing for variable content volumes without knowing the final copy. Second: ensuring a smooth user journey despite fluctuating content required flexible sections that could be rearranged based on the information provided.

This adaptable design approach ensures the website gracefully handles any amount of information, delivering a consistently engaging experience for all visitors.

Design 03

The Solution

Embracing boldness: a sophisticated dark palette creates a dramatic backdrop, drawing users in with sharp contrast against the brand's vibrant red. This color harmony enhances the website's visual impact and reflects the company's boldness.

Dynamic content and interactions bring the product's fast-paced nature to life. Meticulously adhering to the provided brand guidelines — including consistent fonts — ensures a seamless connection between the website and the established brand image, fostering trust and strengthening user recognition.

NDV Performance — final website design

Prototyping 04

Testing & Validation

The flexible layout system was validated by running multiple content scenarios through the design — from minimal copy states to fully loaded pages. Each scenario confirmed the layout maintained its visual integrity and narrative flow without requiring manual adjustment.

Refinement 05

Iteration & Polish

Refinement focused on ensuring brand consistency at every breakpoint and content volume. Color, typography, and spacing were carefully calibrated to honor the NDV brand while pushing the design to feel premium and performance-oriented.

Impact 06

The Outcome

NDV Performance gives you the education and training to gain a competitive edge to become an elite professional. The website redesign embodies the client's brand identity — capturing the dynamic, high-performance nature of their training philosophy.

The flexible layout system ensured the design remained consistent and engaging regardless of how content evolved over time — delivering a website that truly reflected the brand's essence.
