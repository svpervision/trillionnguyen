The Story

Born Between Cultures,
Designing for Humans.

I was born in sunny Southern California, but my story took a detour early on when my family moved to the Philippines. The vibrant culture and tight-knit communities of my childhood left an indelible mark on me. When we returned to the U.S., I faced a new challenge: acclimating to the social norms of American life. I often felt like an outsider, caught between two worlds — but this struggle became the catalyst for something extraordinary.

Design became my way to communicate — to express myself when words felt insufficient. It was a language that transcended cultural barriers, allowing me to connect, share, and create. Growing up in California, I was surrounded by the ever-present pulse of fashion, art, and music. These influences fueled my desire to design not just objects, but experiences that evoke emotion and resonate deeply.

With this passion driving me, I earned a degree in Graphic Design and naturally gravitated toward product design — drawn by curiosity, a desire to help people, and the challenge of designing things that actually work for the humans using them.

Over the years I've helped create intuitive, scalable tools for athletes, schools, and brands. I've collaborated with cross-functional teams to bring meaningful designs to life — designs that simplify complexity, empower users, and drive impact. For me, every project is a chance to challenge myself, grow, and craft something truly meaningful.

I'm also a proud parent to two daughters who inspire me daily. I want them to see the value of hard work, creativity, and resilience — and to know that their mom played a role in shaping the products they interact with.
